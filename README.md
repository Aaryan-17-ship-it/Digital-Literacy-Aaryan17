Task-1 Infographic
<br>
<br>
I made a single page infographic using Canva for this task. I picked Canva because it is easy to use and helps me make something that looks professional. I wanted to show my students that we can make something good without spending money. So I only used the pictures and icons that Canva has.

My infographic is called "BE A SMART STUDENT: YOUR DIGITAL LITERACY GUIDE." It talks three main things: what digital literacy is, how to be safe on the internet and how to write a good email. I used colors to make it easy to see the important things like using Two-Factor Authentication and starting an email in the right way.

The hard part was figuring out what to put in and what to leave out. I had to make sure it was not too messy but still had information. I learned how to use space and pictures to help people read it easily. I found this part really interesting because it was a challenge. I had to decide what was important and what was not.

I think making this infographic was an experience. I learned a lot, about literacy and how to make something that is easy to read. My infographic "BE A SMART STUDENT: YOUR DIGITAL LITERACY GUIDE " is something that I'm proud of.
